Our Available Dogs

Delilah

Delilah is a sweet Chihuahua that likes people but needs some time to warm up to them. As soon as she knows them though she wants to be their best friend and cuddle buddy. She can be a bit bossy around other dogs but likes other young females to play and romp around with.

Delilah loves to chase her toys and is just learning to walk on a leash; she is still a bit skittish when around cars and bicycles. Delilah is housebroken and would like to do her business on walks or in a fenced secured yard.

With 9 lbs. she is a bit chubby and could stand to lose a couple of pounds. Delilah is 3 years old, she is spayed, up to date on all vaccines and she has a microchip.




Faith

Faith is a mellow, sweet Chihuahua mix. She likes everyone she meets; she has a lot of love to give and is a great cuddle buddy. Faith loves to go on walks and is very well behaved on the leash what makes her a great companion to take on everyday errands.

She likes other dogs but she can also be an only dog. She seldom barks and is housebroken.

Faith is 8 years old, she weighs 20 lbs., she is spayed, up to date on all vaccines, she has a microchip and recently had a dental.
